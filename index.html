Origins

Zachary Spann was born on the east coast and has lived in Arizona, Alaska, Honduras, and the Netherlands, but grew up in Oregon.
After he graduated high school Zack moved to Washington for college, and eventually to San Francisco, where he dropped out of Law School.

Zack is a very active and healthy guy. He loves rock climbing, hiking, sailing, and biking (when it's not raining).
